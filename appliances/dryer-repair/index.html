Heating Element Burnout

LG electric dryer heating element repair addresses the most common cause of no-heat complaints. The heating element consists of coiled wire that glows red-hot when electricity passes through it. Over time, these coils can break from thermal stress or develop shorts to the housing. When the element fails, your dryer tumbles but produces no heat. Our technicians test element continuity, inspect for visible breaks, and install genuine LG replacement elements rated for your specific model throughout Newark, Jersey City, and New Jersey.

Thermal Fuse Failure

LG electric dryer thermal fuse repair resolves a common safety device failure that stops dryer operation completely. The thermal fuse is a one-time safety device that blows when the dryer overheats—once blown, it cannot be reset. Common causes include clogged vents restricting airflow, failed thermostats, and cycling thermostat issues. Our technicians replace blown thermal fuses AND diagnose why overheating occurred to prevent repeat failures. Thermal fuse service throughout Edison, Woodbridge, and New Jersey.

High-Limit Thermostat Trips

LG electric dryer high-limit thermostat repair addresses the safety device that cuts power when temperatures exceed safe levels. Unlike the thermal fuse, the high-limit thermostat can reset—but repeated trips indicate underlying problems. Restricted airflow from clogged vents, failed cycling thermostats, or heating element issues cause overheating. Our technicians test thermostat function, identify the overheating cause, and repair all contributing factors throughout Elizabeth, Trenton, and New Jersey homes.

Timer Motor Failures

LG electric dryer timer repair fixes the mechanical or electronic timers that sequence dry cycles. Timer problems cause cycles to stop mid-dry, run continuously without advancing, or skip portions of the program. Mechanical timers have internal motors and contact points that wear over time. Electronic timers on control boards can have component failures. Our technicians diagnose timer issues and replace failed components, restoring proper cycle sequencing throughout Paterson, Clifton, and New Jersey.

Belt Breakage

LG electric dryer belt replacement restores drum rotation when the drive belt breaks. The drum belt wraps around the drum and connects to the motor via an idler pulley. Belts wear, crack, and eventually break from normal use. When the belt breaks, the motor runs but the drum doesn't turn. Belt replacement is a common repair our technicians complete quickly, typically including idler pulley inspection since worn pulleys accelerate belt wear. Belt service throughout Dumont, New Milford, and New Jersey.

Drum Roller Wear

LG electric dryer drum roller repair eliminates the thumping, squeaking, or rumbling sounds caused by worn drum support rollers. Most dryers use two to four rollers supporting the drum's rear and sometimes front. When roller bearings wear or flat spots develop, you'll hear rhythmic noise during tumbling. Our technicians replace drum rollers as a set, ensuring balanced support and quiet operation. Drum roller service throughout New Jersey electric dryer installations.

Door Switch Problems

LG electric dryer door switch repair fixes the safety mechanism that prevents operation when the door is open. The door switch signals the control board that the door is closed—if it fails, the dryer won't start even with the door shut. Switch contacts wear from repeated door opening and closing. Our technicians test switch continuity and replace failed switches, restoring safe operation throughout Tenafly, Cresskill, and New Jersey homes.

Control Board Issues

LG electric dryer control board repair addresses electronic failures affecting dryer operation. The main control board manages all functions—accepting user inputs, controlling heating, monitoring sensors, and timing cycles. Board failures cause erratic behavior, error codes, cycles not completing, or complete failure to operate. Power surges and component failures are common causes. Our technicians diagnose board problems accurately, replacing boards when necessary throughout Wyckoff, Franklin Lakes, and New Jersey.

Motor Failures

LG electric dryer motor repair fixes the drive motor that rotates the drum and powers the blower wheel. Motor failures cause the dryer not to run at all, run intermittently, or make humming noises without turning. Motors can fail from bearing wear, winding shorts, or start switch problems. Our technicians diagnose motor issues, determining whether the motor itself has failed or if other components like the belt or idler are binding the motor. Motor service throughout New Jersey.

Terminal Block Damage

LG electric dryer terminal block repair addresses the connection point where the power cord attaches to the dryer. Terminal blocks can become damaged from loose connections causing arcing and burning, improper cord installation, or physical damage. Damaged terminal blocks are a fire hazard requiring immediate repair. Our technicians inspect connections, replace damaged blocks, and ensure secure, proper power cord installation throughout Newark, Jersey City, and New Jersey.

240V Connection Issues

LG electric dryer 240V troubleshooting diagnoses power supply problems affecting dryer operation. Electric dryers require 240-volt power—if only 120 volts reaches the dryer, it will run but not heat. Causes include tripped breakers, blown fuses, outlet problems, and cord damage. Our technicians test voltage at the outlet and throughout the dryer circuit, identifying power supply issues that may require electrician service or dryer repairs we can complete throughout New Jersey.

Gas Valve Solenoid Failures

LG gas dryer valve solenoid repair addresses one of the most common gas dryer failures. Gas valve solenoids are electromagnetic coils that open the gas valve when the igniter gets hot enough. Most LG gas dryers have two solenoids—one for the main valve and one for the secondary. These solenoids can fail intermittently, working when cold but failing when hot. Our technicians diagnose solenoid failures using amp draw testing and replace failed coils throughout Newark, Jersey City, and New Jersey.

Igniter Problems

LG gas dryer igniter repair fixes the component that lights the gas burner. The igniter glows orange-hot, reaching temperatures high enough to ignite gas. When igniters weaken, they glow but don't get hot enough to open the gas valve or ignite the gas. Completely failed igniters don't glow at all. Our technicians test igniter amp draw—weak igniters draw less current even while glowing. Igniter replacement restores reliable heating throughout Edison, Woodbridge, and New Jersey.

Flame Sensor Malfunctions

LG gas dryer flame sensor repair addresses the safety device that monitors burner operation. The flame sensor detects heat from the burner flame—if it doesn't sense heat, it shuts off gas flow as a safety measure. Failed sensors can incorrectly shut off gas even when flames are present, causing intermittent heating. Sensors also fail by not detecting flame absence, though this is less common. Our technicians test and replace flame sensors throughout Elizabeth, Trenton, and New Jersey.

Burner Assembly Clogs

LG gas dryer burner cleaning removes lint and debris that accumulate in the burner assembly. Unlike lint in the exhaust system, burner area lint affects combustion quality and can be a fire hazard. Clogged burner ports cause uneven flames, yellow flames (instead of blue), and incomplete combustion. Our technicians clean burner assemblies, inspect gas orifices, and ensure proper combustion throughout Paterson, Clifton, and New Jersey gas dryer installations.

Gas Line Issues

LG gas dryer gas line troubleshooting identifies supply problems affecting dryer operation. Low gas pressure, kinked flexible connectors, and partially closed shutoff valves can prevent proper burner operation. Gas leaks in connections are serious safety concerns requiring immediate attention. Our technicians test gas pressure at the dryer, inspect connections for leaks using proper detection methods, and ensure safe gas supply throughout Dumont, New Milford, and New Jersey homes.

Safety Thermostat Trips

LG gas dryer safety thermostat repair addresses the temperature-limiting devices that protect against overheating. Gas dryers have high-limit thermostats and thermal fuses that stop operation when temperatures exceed safe levels. Repeated trips indicate airflow restrictions, usually from clogged vents or lint-blocked internal passages. Our technicians replace failed safety devices AND identify why overheating occurred, preventing repeat failures throughout New Jersey.

No Heat Complaints

LG gas dryer no heat diagnosis systematically identifies why your dryer tumbles without producing heat. Gas dryer no-heat problems can stem from igniter failure, solenoid failure, flame sensor issues, thermal fuse failure, clogged vents, or gas supply problems. Our technicians follow diagnostic procedures to identify the specific cause, testing components in logical sequence. Accurate diagnosis ensures we repair the right part the first time throughout Tenafly, Cresskill, and New Jersey.

Intermittent Heating

LG gas dryer intermittent heat repair fixes inconsistent heating that causes some loads to dry while others don't. Intermittent problems are often more challenging to diagnose than complete failures. Common causes include failing solenoid coils that work when cold but fail when hot, loose connections, and marginal igniters. Our technicians use extended testing and thermal monitoring to catch intermittent failures, providing reliable repairs throughout Wyckoff, Franklin Lakes, and New Jersey.

Gas Smell Concerns

LG gas dryer gas odor inspection addresses safety concerns about gas smells. Mild gas odor during ignition is normal as unburned gas briefly escapes before lighting. Persistent gas smell indicates potential leaks in connections, valves, or supply lines requiring immediate attention. Strong gas odor means evacuate and call your gas company. Our technicians perform thorough leak detection using approved methods, ensuring safe gas dryer operation throughout New Jersey homes.

Combustion Testing

LG gas dryer combustion analysis verifies proper burner operation and complete gas combustion. Proper combustion produces clean, blue flames and minimal carbon monoxide. Yellow flames, sooting, and incomplete combustion indicate adjustment or cleaning needs. Our technicians inspect flame characteristics, check burner alignment, and ensure exhaust gases vent properly. Complete combustion testing throughout Alpine, Closter, and New Jersey gas dryer service calls.

Pilot Light Issues

LG gas dryer pilot system service addresses older models with standing pilot lights or newer electronic ignition systems. While most modern LG dryers use electric ignition, understanding your ignition system type is essential for proper diagnosis. Our technicians service all ignition system types, from standing pilots to hot surface igniters to spark ignition systems. Complete ignition system service throughout New Jersey for all LG gas dryer models.
